# Quotes

Quotes are statements that were said by a person, but famous quotes often get a place, especially when it comes to being inspirational.

***

## <mark style="color:$primary;">Usage</mark>

If you want to get quotes, use one of the following functions.

<details>

<summary>Functions</summary>

<table><thead><tr><th width="169.6666259765625">Function</th><th>Description</th></tr></thead><tbody><tr><td><code>GetAllQuotes()</code></td><td>Gets the whole quote list.</td></tr><tr><td><code>GetRandomQuote()</code></td><td>Gets a random quote.</td></tr><tr><td><code>GetQuote()</code></td><td>Gets a specified quote by number.</td></tr></tbody></table>

A quote instance contains the following properties:

<table><thead><tr><th width="119.6666259765625">Property</th><th>Description</th></tr></thead><tbody><tr><td><code>Author</code></td><td>Author of the quote</td></tr><tr><td><code>Content</code></td><td>Quote content</td></tr></tbody></table>

</details>
